The Mission

Military branches exist to protect Americans from external threats. The United States Medical Force (USMF) would protect Americans from the threats that actually impact us every day: illness, healthcare shortages, and disasters.

USMF is a proposed uniformed, non-combat national service designed to provide:

  • Baseline Public Care Capacity: A guaranteed network of clinics and providers
  • Emergency Readiness: Rapid deployment for pandemics and disasters
  • Medical Workforce Development: Debt-free education in exchange for service
